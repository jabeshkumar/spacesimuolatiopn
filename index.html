Black Holes

A Black Hole is a regoin in space where the gravity is so strong that not even light can escape it's force! It is made when a Star dies, a Supernova.

Fun Fact:

A Black hole may be an opening to a "Worm hole"! (The other end is called a White Hole)

Black Hole Facts:

- You Can't Directly See a Black Hole.
- Our Milky Way Probably Has a Black Hole.
- Dying Stars Lead to Stellar Black Holes.
- The First Black Hole Wasn't Discovered Until X-Ray Astronomy was Used.
- A Black Hole can kill a person
- Black holes are the only objects in the Universe that can trap light by sheer gravitational force.
- Black holes could spawn new universes.
- Black holes literally pull the space around them.
- Black holes slow down time.
- Black holes evaporate over time.
- Black holes are the ultimate energy factories.
